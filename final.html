A growing body of evidence is gradually revealing the extent of the burden that alcohol places upon our public services. Up to 80% of weekend arrests are alcohol-related 1 , and just over half of violent crime is committed under the influence 2. In 2009/10 there were 1.4 million alcohol-related ambulance journeys, which represents 35% of the overall total 3.

This map show the locations of vehicle accidents related to alcohol within the City of Albuquerque. There are two options to compare, 2008 Accidents and 2009 Accidents including the locations of Alcohol Establishments. Accident locations are not just one accident but include more than one accident at that location. So each of the dots are just an indication where the locations are.

  • 2009 Accidents = Red Triangles
  • 2008 Accidents = Blue Triangles
  • Establishments = Black Squares
