Question 1

What area do you want to depict in your map?

    Elena Gallegos Park

Why?

    This is an area of Albuquerque where I tend to distance myself on the weekends from the city.
    Either hiking up through the canyon or mountain biking the lower trails, your just far enough away that you cannot hear anything but nature.

Question 2

What is the center point (latitude and longitude) of your area of interest?

    LAT: 35.162933 & LONG: -106.469986

Question 3

What base map(s) did you select for use in your map?

    I used 3 maps: Open Street Map, Google Street and Google Terrain map.

Why?

    I wanted to keep the map simple and familiar for the end user with the street maps.
    I also added the terrain map so the user can get an idea of elevation throughout the park.
